Purpose

This portal allows citizens to evaluate hospitals across Somalia. The collected ratings help hospitals improve healthcare services and inform patients of quality care.

Mission

To create a reliable system for tracking hospital performance and improving healthcare standards in Somalia.

Vision

To become the leading digital platform for healthcare evaluation and patient feedback in Somalia.
